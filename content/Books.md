# The Book of Galactic Light
This book is the record of my inquiry into Enochian Magic as a means for human initiated contact with the Enochian Angels, which I presumed at the time to be ET or NHI and possibly related to the UAP phenomenon. 

The Book of Galactic Light is especially for you if you are interested in Enochian Magic, want to know how Western Esoteric traditions inform contemporary UAP Disclosure narratives, or feel the call to initiation, shamanism, or ritual and ceremonial magic.
# Missing Time Found
This book is a scholarly presentation of the shamanic dreaming hypothesis of NHI/UAP missing time, framed as a transpersonal psychology and integral studies.  The hypothesis is central to my dreamwork practice, which offers shamanic dreamwork as an alternative to regression hypnosis for  the recovery of memories like alien abduction.  The book documents my experiences as an experiencer and integral researcher, including the investigation of my own missing time event remembered, corroborated, and hypnotically explored during the course of writing.

It is my contribution to the literary tradition of hypnotist-researchers documenting dreamlike alien encounters that began with Budd Hopkins, the modern artist turned abduction writer, who authored Missing Time in 1981.  

Missing Time Found is especially for you if you have had a ET/NHI or UFO/UAP missing time event, you feel curious yet cautious about hypnosis, or you want to understand 1990s alien abduction research evolved into 2020s UAP disclosure narratives.
# Galethog the Grey's Field Guide to Anomalous Geometry
This book documents my methodological and integral inquiry into body marks as evidence for alien abduction.  Alien abduction researchers like Hopkins suggested that unexplained marks on the body accompanied by dreams or intuitions of alien encounters supports extraordinary claims. My inquiry into body marks arose upon the discovery of my own anomalous geometric body marks, synchronistic with a series of strange dreams and the completion of the Missing Time Found draft. 

Galethog is the name of an Enochian Angel, which I playfully gave to the NHI character I encountered in my Missing Time Found regression and body mark dreams.  I collected particularly interesting samples of several body mark types that I had previously encountered and studied associated with abduction or encounter reports, producing statement aligned to Wilber's AQAL model as a method.  I then channeled a chapter of the book from Galethog's perspective to make meaning from the collection.

Galethog the Grey's Field Guide to Anomalous Geometry is especially for you if you have had an anomalous body mark associated with a dream, memory, or intuition of encounter or if you have been touched by 1990s alien abduction narratives.
# DSETI Research Institute Proposal
This book is a proposal for a research institute supported by my academic writing.  The book is dedicated to Robert Bigelow and Hans-Adam Liechtenstein, who funded the 1990s alien abduction research that inspired my interest in dreams and ET contact.  It is a literary response to a public invitation Robert Bigelow published in the 1992 Unusual Personal Experiences booklet.  While I sent Bigelow these book and never heard back, this book is meant for an lay person interested in the literary connections between 1990s abduction research and contemporary UAP disclosure and NHI encounter narratives.

DSETI Research Institute Proposal is especially for you if you care about literary connections between abduction research and UAP disclosure or you want to operationalize DSETI methods similar to remote viewing.
